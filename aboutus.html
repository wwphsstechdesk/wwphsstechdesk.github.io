About Us

Who We Are

We are a group of students from West Windsor-Plainsboro High School South looking to enhance the technological literacy of our community by lending a hand to whomever is in need of tech help. The Tech Desk operates in the Media Center throughout the day, including lunch and even after school while the library is open. Other than simply showing up to the library, volunteers are proactive in helping peers, including other library volunteers. Occasionally, the club partners with other clubs to build websites and holds workshops with new gadgets to be tinkered with.

Mission Statement

With programming and the internet in general so prevalent today, especially now that more students have access to technology through chromebooks in their daily lives, the HSS Tech Desk aims to dispel any technological doubts anyone may have.
